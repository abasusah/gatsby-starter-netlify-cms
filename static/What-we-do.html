WHAT WE DO

Ticketing 
(Local and International):
MAINWORLD offers th​e best fares as we partner with various travel key players all over the world.

Foreign Admissions
(Student Placement):
Our educational service desk is fully functional and established to meet the demands of students in need of quality education abroad. We represent high-profile institutions and universities globally.

Hotel Reservation and Accomodation(Local and International):
We take the burden off our clients by looking for oppurtunities and booking affordable hotels, resorts and apartments. We use top rated quality hotels and have up to 50% discount in various five-star hotels globally. 

Travel Insurance:
We facilitate travel insurance covers to take care of unforseen circumstances that may occur durung international trips. The package covers health and losses durning the duration of the trip.

Charter Services:
We facilitate these services through the provision of private jets, commercial planes, yacht, boat cruise, luxury buses/luxury cars on request.

Car Rentals:
We facilitate round-trip airport pickup and drop off; corporate car hires with armed escorts (on request); car hire covering states and regions - all tailored to suit the needs of our clients - both corporate and individual.

Visa Procurement Assistance:
We facilitate prompt visa processing /procurement by assisting clients with required documentation where provided.

Vacation/Holiday Packages:
We help our clients plan exciting vacations and holiday packages, as well as tours, to any destination of their choice - working within budget to give them more fulfilling journeys and more cherished memories.

Tourism
(Medical, Educational, Sports, Business & Cultural Tours):
We package tours on all the and improve travel and tour experience. We facilitate logistics and related support to tourists travelling to any part of the world for either medical, educa​​ti​onal, sports, business and/or cultural tours.

Conferences/Seminars:
We facilitate participating opportunities for organisations, ministries, departments and agencies to take their staff on conferences of trainings in various fields, in any part of the world. We also offer MICE (Meeting Incentives, Conferences and Exhibition) services, which may comprise of language services and interpretation during zoom or any virtual/physical meetings.

BDC Services:
We offer Bureau De Change Services in various currencies and can facilitate legal receipt, payments

General Consultancy:
We offer consultancy on tourism and hospitality services
